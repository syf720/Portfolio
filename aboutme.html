I think that robots are still really cool, but we need to be careful in case they ever take over the worlds. Working in groups is fun because if you have a problem, they can help you. Manuela Veloso and Ayanna Howard's work are in common because they both work with robots. My team worked really well together on the lightshow. We could have improved by adding more extensions. The basic pieces of every Arduino sketch are the setup and a loop that tells the robot what to do. The pin output is really important otherwise the actions won't work.
